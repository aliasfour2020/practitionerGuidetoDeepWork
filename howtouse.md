# How to Use

## Slack

Foreach workspace change your Do Not Disturb settings.

1. Click the workspace name in the top left.
2. Click Preferences.
3. Scroll to Do Not Disturb section
4. Change `Automatically disable notifications from:`
    * 5:00 pm to 8:00 am
